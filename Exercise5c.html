Fruit Graham Cake

Description

It is a quick and easy dessert dish that you can whip-up anytime. It does not involve baking and it is so easy to make. I recommend this dish for beginners because of its simplicity. You should be able to make this dish without any issues as long as you follow our step by step recipe. This is best to eat when it is refrigerated.

 

Ingredients:

  • 12 pcs Graham Brackers, Crushed
  • 1/4 cup Melted Butter
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 pack 125ml NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream
  • 1/2 small can Condensed Milk
  • 1 can fruit cocktail, Drained well
 

Instructions:

  1. Mix graham crackers, butter, and sugar. Lightly press at the bottom of a serving container.
  2. Whip NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream and mix with condensed milk.
  3. Assemble fruit cocktail and sweetened cream in layers. Refrigerate overnight. Serve well chilled.
