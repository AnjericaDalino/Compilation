Chicken Liver Adobo

Description

Chicken Liver Adobo or locally known in the Philippines as Adobong Atay ng Manok is an appetizing dish that is not only delicious but also easy to make. This dish is the same as the basic Filipino adobo recipe cooked in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves and whole peppercorn. The secret to a delicious Chicken Liver Adobo is the extra garlic and sugar but sugar is always optional. Liver taste great if cooked and seasoned perfectly. This dish is must a try dish because it is really tasty.

 

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Chicken Liver
  • 1 head Garlic
  • 2 tsp Black Peppercorn
  • 6 tbsp White Vinegar
  • 1 pc Bay Leaf
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 cup Water
 

Instructions:

  1. Combine chicken liver, garlic, peppercorn, vinegar, bay leaf soy sauce, sugar and water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Strain and continue to simmer cooking liquid to another 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, sauté chicken liver in oil until golden brown. Pour cooking liquid, season with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®, and continue to simmer for another 2 minutes. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
